About Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a visionary entrepreneur, inventor, and engineer who has made significant contributions to various industries. Born on June 28, 1971, in South Africa, Musk's journey is a testament to his relentless pursuit of innovation and his mission to make the world a better place.
